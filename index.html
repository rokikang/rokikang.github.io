About Me

Hey there, I'm Roki — a student of Economics at Erasmus University Rotterdam. This is a personal page about ideas, thoughts, and books circling around my head.

My central academic enquiry concerns the question: "how do we live well together?"

For that, I enjoy studying and reading about...

Economics: Ecological Economics, Endogenous Utility

Philosophy and Religion: Ethics, Moral & Political Philosophy, Buddhism (Madhyamaka, Zen, Engaged Buddhism)

Psychology: Moral, Social, Developmental, Evolutionary Psychology

with hopes that learning about them will take me, and hopefully our world, to somewhere.
